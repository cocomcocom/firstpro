What Is Apk File:


Da farko dai shi Apk file kunfi saninsa a matsayin app a android.
Sannan nasan kuna yi masa kallon abu daya tak!
Toh kusani shi wannan app din ba abu daya bane a tare dashi, wannan shine muke kira da Apk file.
Shi Apk file tamkar jaka ce wacce take kunshe da littafai da kuma kayan aiki, yayin da aka zuge jakar kawai zakace jakace amma da an bude zakaga abinda take dauke dashi!
Toh haka shima wannan Apk file Wanda kukafi sani da app yake. Saboda haka yanzu zamu zugeshi muga abinda ya kunsa

Unzipping Apk File:


Apk file yanada suna sannan da kuma extension (ma'ana wani karin karamar kalmar a karshen sunansa bayan ansaka dugo ".") shi extension dinsa shine sunan app din da kuma ".apk" kamar "Game.apk" ko "Ludo.apk". A zahiri idan mukayi clicking din app zai fara installing ne a wayarmu, toh idan muna san muyi shi wannan Unzipping (Zuge app mu bude shi), se munbi wadannan steps din:-

Step-1
Zamu yi renaming din shi wannan extension din nasa na .apk to .zip

Step-2
Zamuyi long pressing din app din har seya bamu options.

Step-3
Zamuyi sellecting din "decompress" ko "extract" a list din options din daya bamu. A wata wayar yana bada option din "decompress" a wata kuma "extract", toh ko wannene ma zaiyi.

Step-4
Zamuyi sellecting din folder wacce mukeso ya ajiye mana budadden app din.
